Flights Delayed During Departure and Arrival

From the line chart below we see that flights are more likely to be delayed when Christmas is close.

Select an interval in the line chart to filter out the dates in the maps.

Flights departing from ATL were delayed by at least 5 minutes on average. The most delayed flights during departure in 2015 were flights that were travelling to John F. Kennedy International Airport in New York, being delayed by 17 minutes on average.

Flights arriving in ATL are usually on time. However, there are a some flights that actually arrived later than the scheduled arrival time and a few flights that arrived earlier than the scheduled time on average.
