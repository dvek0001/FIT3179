US FLIGHT DELAYS AND CANCELLATION

Flights Delayed During Departure and Arrival

Flights departing from Hartsfield-Jackson Atlanta International Airport (ATL) were delayed more on the day of Chrismas Eve (Decemeber 24). On this day, flights departed 38 minutes later than scheduled on average and arrived 35 minutes later than scheduled.

Flights departing from ATL are delayed by at least 5 minutes on average. However, it is possible that the delay during arrival (Arrival Delay) is less than the delay during departure (Departure Delay). In Decemeber, flights travelling to Seattle-Tacoma International Airport (SEA) departed 15 minutes later than the scheduled departure time, but they arrived 10 minutes later than the scheduled arrival.
